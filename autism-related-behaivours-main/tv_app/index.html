⚠️ Important Disclaimer

Early Screen ASD is an assistive tool designed to help identify potential signs of autism spectrum disorder in children through video analysis. This tool is NOT a diagnostic tool and should not be used as a substitute for professional medical evaluation.

Important: Always consult with qualified healthcare professionals for accurate diagnosis and treatment recommendations.

What is Early Screen ASD?

Early Screen ASD uses advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze video footage of children's behavior and identify patterns that may be associated with autism spectrum disorder. The system is designed to assist healthcare professionals and caregivers in the early identification process.

How It Works

  1. Upload: Submit a video of the child's natural behavior
  2. Analysis: Our AI system processes the video frame by frame
  3. Detection: The system identifies behavioral patterns and movements
  4. Report: Generate a detailed analysis report for professional review

What We Analyze

✋

Hand Movements

Repetitive hand flapping, finger movements, and unusual hand positioning

🔄

Body Movements

Spinning, rocking, and other repetitive body movements

👀

Eye Contact

Patterns of gaze and eye contact behavior

🗣️

Vocal Patterns

Speech patterns and vocalizations

Benefits of Early Detection

Early identification of autism spectrum disorder can lead to:

  • Earlier access to intervention services
  • Improved long-term outcomes
  • Better support for families and caregivers
  • More effective educational planning

Privacy & Security

We take your privacy seriously. All video uploads are:

  • Encrypted during transmission and storage
  • Processed locally when possible
  • Automatically deleted after analysis
  • Never shared without explicit consent
